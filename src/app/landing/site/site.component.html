Frequently Asked Questions

arrow_drop_down_circle How do I get started with The List of Shame?

Simply register a new account for free if you don't already have one by clicking GET STARTED . If you already have an account, just click LOGIN and provide your credentials.

arrow_drop_down_circle Are there any charges for using this platform?

Viewing and searching for any debtors is absolutely FREE. Listing a debtor is also free but in order to publish the listing for the outside world to see, you will be charged 3% of the amount owed. You will also be charged 7% of the amount owed in order to unlist the debtor.

arrow_drop_down_circle Why should I pay to list someone?

In order to ensure that only authentic information is provided via this platform, we charge a levy amounting to 10% of the owed amount both for listing and unlisting a debtor. This will discourage providing any false information that might be posted for malicious purposes.

arrow_drop_down_circle What is the procedure for unlisting someone?

To unlist someone, simply click on the debtor and follow the instructions to pay the unlisting fee indicated. After successful payment, the debtor will be unlisted.

arrow_drop_down_circle Are there any legal issues that might arise from using this platform?

We highly encorage you to provide authentic information via this platform. Any false information might result in legal issues and we clearly state in the terms and conditions that we shall not be held liable in the event of such an occurence.

arrow_drop_down_circle Is my data safe? And will you expose my information when I list someone?

User data is 100% safe. We value data privacy and we DO NOT expose your information when you list someone.
